Uncommon Sabers

Sabers require Strength to use, and have the highest low-end base damage of the four weapon types. Sabers have an attack speed of 2-1. Uncommon Sabers may be sold in stores or dropped by enemies, but are considered 'already forged' and cannot have additional comps forged to them. This is a list of the Uncommon Sabers available in stores.
