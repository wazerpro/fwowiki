Uncommon Staff/Spears

Staves and Spears require Agility to use, and have the lowest high end base damage. Staves and Spears have an attack speed of 2-2. Uncommon Staves and Spears may be sold in stores or dropped by enemies, but are considered 'already forged' and cannot have additional comps forged to them. This is a list of the Uncommon Staves and Spears available in stores.
