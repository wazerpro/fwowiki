Common Equipment

Sold in stores and dropped by mobs, common types of equipment are easy to come by. Generally most common equipment is not that good and only used when nothing better is available.

Common Amulets
  Name Lvl/LvlRange Description
Striking Amulet 1-10, 15,20..100 Adds a small amount of AR.
Level 1 adds +4 AR, Lv5 is at +12 AR, and every 5 levels after adds +11 AR.
Example: Lv40 = AR +89
Deflecting Amulet 1-10, 15,20..100 Adds a small amount of DR.
Level 1 adds +3 DR, Lv5 is at +12 DR, and every 5 levels after adds +9 DR.
Example: Lv30 = +61 DR
Health Amulet 1,5,10, 15,20..100 Adds to Max HP by a small amount.
Level 1 adds +10 HP, Lv5 is at +14 HP, and every 5 levels after adds +5 HP.
Example: Lv40 = +49 Max HP
Energy Amulet 1,5,10, 15,20..100 Adds to Max Chi by a small amount.
Level 1 adds +12 Chi, Level 5 is at +16 Chi, and every 5 levels after adds +5 Chi.
Example: Lv25 = +36 Max Chi

Notes:
Generally, these common amulets are not useful for much other than selling. During the low levels they can be useful, but quickly become useless as their small stat increase does not keep up at all as you get higher. The Striking Amulets are useful until around the mid 30s, mainly for providing YY alts with some improvement to their hit rate.
