Shop and Trainer Lists

This section is a list of the store inventories of each region. Prices should be taken as an approximate figure, rather than an exact number, especially in the towns in the 'Land of' maps as the prices here are determined by which clan controls a town, your clan, how many towns that clan has, etc (during the times I worked on this, towns were in varying states of ownership). There are also other things that can affect prices, such as pk penalty and the presence of clan relics in their home locations, among others. This section will be updated with information on shop items not available on Fire Kirin server shortly.
