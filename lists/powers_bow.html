Bow Stance

Can use the best and cheapest long ranged powers, and has very accurate attacks. Users of this stance learn to aim and strike accurately, but have weak points. The bow allows its user to attack at a distance. Bow Stance uses lightning and fire based powers primarily, and cold as a secondary. Power costs listed in Orange are the modified costs for those powers in the Five Clans Server.
