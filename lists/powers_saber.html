Saber Stance

Focused on doing massive damage with a single blow, users of this stance learn how to maximize damage when attacking their opponents. Uses damage enhancing powers. Saber Stance uses cold, fire and physical based powers. Power costs listed in Orange are the modified costs for those powers in the Five Clans Server.
