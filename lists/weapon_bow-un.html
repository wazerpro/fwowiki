Uncommon Bows

Bows are the weapon with the longest range, and require Perception to use. Bows have the highest top-end base damage of the four weapon types. Bows have an attack speed of 1-1. Uncommon Bows may be sold in stores or dropped by enemies, but are considered 'already forged' and cannot have additional comps forged to them. This is a list of the Uncommon Bows available in stores.
