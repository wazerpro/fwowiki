Staff Stance

Providing extra protection from attacks and extra toughness, users of this stance learn great defense but poor attacks. Has defense based powers. Staff Stance uses poison, lightning and physical based powers. Power costs listed in Orange are the modified costs for those powers in the Five Clans Server.
