Except for a few that were posted on the SRO forum, the rest are all from recipes I've seen in game. It should be noted however, that some recipes are 'locked' on Fire Kirin server, meaning that although the recipe exists, if you try to craft a locked item you will get a message that the item is locked and cannot be spawned in game. Recipes with an asterisk (*) in front of the name are confirmed locked.
