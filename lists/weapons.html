Bows

Bows are the weapon with the longest range, and require Perception to use. Bows have the highest top-end base damage of the four weapon types. Bows have an attack speed of 1-1.

Sabers

Sabers require Strength to use, and have the highest low-end base damage of the four weapon types. Sabers have an attack speed of 2-1.

Staff/Spears

Staves and Spears require Agility to use, and have the lowest high end base damage. Staves and Spears have an attack speed of 2-2.

Swords

Swords require both Perception and Agility to use, and their base damage is neither especially high nor especially low. Swords have an attack speed of 2-1.

Rare Weapons

Better than normal weapons, Rare Weapons are great but also extremely difficult to come by. This is a list of all the rare weapons in the game.

Boundless Weapon Sets

Boundless weapons are rare forgeable weapons with increased Base Damage and low stat requirements. They are each part of an equipment set which have various beneficial effects when 3 or 4 set pieces are equipped. All set effects in the game have been disabled due to a stacking bug, but the weapons and much of the equipment are still useful even without the set bonuses.

Uncommon Weapon Types

Many uncommon weapon types are sold in stores, but there are also types that can only be obtained through drops from enemies. This list covers both the types available in stores and those available only as drops.

Uncommon Bows

Uncommon Bows may be sold in stores or dropped by enemies, but are considered 'already forged' and cannot have additional comps forged to them. This is a list of the Uncommon Bows available in stores.

Uncommon Sabers

Uncommon Sabers may be sold in stores or dropped by enemies, but are considered 'already forged' and cannot have additional comps forged to them. This is a list of the Uncommon Sabers available in stores.

Uncommon Staff/Spears

Uncommon Staves and Spears may be sold in stores or dropped by enemies, but are considered 'already forged' and cannot have additional comps forged to them. This is a list of the Uncommon Staves and Spears available in stores.

Uncommon Swords

Uncommon Swords may be sold in stores or dropped by enemies, but are considered 'already forged' and cannot have additional comps forged to them. This is a list of the Uncommon Swords available in stores.
